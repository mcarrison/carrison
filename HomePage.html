Restaurant Description:
Colt's Fine Diner is a family owned and operated eatery which has been a pillar of the community for the last several decades offering family foods at reasonable prices with a fully stocked bar which has local beers on tap. We are open 24 hours a day and seven days a week, and offer breakfast all day, lunch and dinner.
